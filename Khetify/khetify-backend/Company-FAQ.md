# Khetify — Company Section FAQ (100 Questions & Answers)

## 🏢 Registration & Onboarding

**1. Khetify par company account kaise banaye?**
Home page par "Register as Company" button par click karke registration form bharein — company name, email, phone aur password dekar account create ho jata hai.

**2. Company register karne ke liye kya documents chahiye?**
Business name, GST number, trade license, contact details aur bank/PAN details onboarding ke steps me maange jaate hain.

**3. Registration free hai kya?**
Account banana free hai. Kuch premium features subscription plan par depend karte hain (Billing section dekhein).

**4. Ek email se kitne company accounts ban sakte hain?**
Ek email address par ek hi company account allowed hai. Alag company ke liye alag email use karein.

**5. Registration ke baad kya hota hai?**
Aapka application review ke liye jata hai. Approve hone par aap CompanyDashboard access kar paate hain.

**6. Registration approval me kitna time lagta hai?**
Aam taur par 24 ghante ke andar setup complete ho jata hai; verification me thoda extra time lag sakta hai.

**7. Meri registration reject kyun hui?**
Galat/incomplete documents, invalid GST, ya duplicate company hone par reject ho sakti hai. Support se sahi reason confirm karein.

**8. Registration ke steps kya-kya hain?**
Basic info → Company setup (Step 2–5) → Documents → Submission → Approval. Har step save hota jata hai.

**9. Setup adhura chhod diya to data save rahega?**
Haan, aap wahi se continue kar sakte hain jahan chhoda tha.

**10. Registration form submit hone ke baad edit kar sakta hoon?**
Approval se pehle kuch details editable hain; approval ke baad changes ke liye support ticket raise karna padta hai.

**11. Kya Khetify khud logistics/payment handle karta hai?**
Nahi. Khetify platform infrastructure, pricing visibility aur network access deta hai. Delivery aur payment companies/sellers khud manage karte hain.

**12. Onboarding me help chahiye to kise contact karein?**
support@khetify.com par mail karein — team 24 ghante me storefront setup me madad karti hai.

## 🗃️ Inventory Management (IMS)

**46. IMS kya hai?**
Intelligent Inventory Management — multiple warehouses me real-time stock tracking, auto-reorder aur expiry management.

**47. Stock levels kaise track hote hain?**
availableStock = online + offline − reserved; har stock change ledger me record hota hai.

**48. Ek se zyada warehouse ka stock sync hota hai?**
Haan, multi-warehouse syncing se sabhi locations ka stock ek jagah dikhta hai.

**49. Batch aur expiry management hai?**
Haan, batch number aur expiry date ke saath stock control hota hai.

**50. Expiry ke pehle alert milta hai?**
Haan, expiry ke近 aane par system alert deta hai taaki wastage kam ho.

**51. Auto-reorder kaise kaam karta hai?**
Stock minimum level se neeche jaane par automatic reorder trigger ho sakta hai.

**52. Stock adjustment kaise karun?**
IMS me manual adjustment option se stock badha/ghata sakte hain — har change log hota hai.

**53. Lots kya hote hain?**
Lots ek batch/consignment ko represent karte hain jise aap track aur trace kar sakte hain.

**54. Inbound (GRN) kaise record karta hoon?**
Inbound section me goods receive karke GRN post karein — stock automatically update hota hai.

**55. Outbound kaise handle hota hai?**
Outbound section se dispatch record karein; stock reserve/deduct ho jata hai.

**56. Stock movement history dekh sakta hoon?**
Haan, har stock change ka append-only ledger maintain hota hai jise delete nahi kiya ja sakta.

**57. Inventory galat dikh raha hai to?**
Movements ledger check karein; discrepancy ho to Support (Inventory & Stock) me report karein.
