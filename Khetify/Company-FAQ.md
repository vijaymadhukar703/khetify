## 🏢 Registration & Onboarding

**1. How do I create a company account on Khetify?**  
Click the **"Register as Company"** button on the home page and complete the registration form by providing your company name, email address, phone number, and password. Once submitted, your company account will be created.

---

**2. What documents are required to register a company?**  
During the onboarding process, you will be asked to provide your business name, GST number, trade license, contact information, and bank/PAN details for verification.

---

**3. Is company registration free?**  
Yes, creating a company account is completely free. However, some advanced features are available only with a premium subscription plan. Please refer to the **Billing** section for more information.

---

**4. How many company accounts can be created using one email address?**  
Only one company account can be registered with a single email address. If you need to register another company, you must use a different email address.

---

**5. What happens after I complete the registration?**  
Your application will be submitted for review. Once it is approved, you will receive access to the Company Dashboard and all available company features.

---

**6. How long does the approval process take?**  
In most cases, the approval process is completed within 24 hours. However, additional verification may require extra processing time.

---

**7. Why was my registration application rejected?**  
Your application may be rejected due to incomplete or incorrect information, an invalid GST number, duplicate company registration, or missing verification documents. Please contact the support team for the exact reason and further assistance.

---

**8. What are the steps involved in the registration process?**  
The registration process consists of the following steps:

1. Enter basic company information.
2. Complete the company setup (Steps 2–5).
3. Upload the required documents.
4. Submit your application.
5. Wait for approval.

Your progress is automatically saved at each step.

---

**9. Will my information be saved if I leave the setup before completing it?**  
Yes. Your progress is automatically saved, allowing you to resume the registration process from where you left off.

---

**10. Can I edit my registration details after submitting the application?**  
Some information can be updated before your application is approved. Once approved, changes to certain details may require submitting a support request.

**11. Does Khetify handle logistics and payments?**  
No. Khetify provides the platform infrastructure, product visibility, and business network. Delivery, logistics, and payment transactions are managed directly between the company and the seller.

---

**12. Who should I contact if I need help during onboarding?**  
If you need assistance during the onboarding process, please contact our support team at **support@khetify.com**. Our team typically responds within 24 hours and will help you complete your storefront setup.

---

## 🗃️ Inventory Management (IMS)

**46. What is IMS?**  
IMS (Intelligent Inventory Management System) is Khetify's inventory management solution that provides real-time stock tracking across multiple warehouses, automated reorder management, and batch and expiry tracking.

---

**47. How are inventory levels calculated?**  
Inventory is calculated using the following formula:

**Available Stock = Online Stock + Offline Stock − Reserved Stock**

Every inventory transaction is recorded in a secure audit ledger for complete traceability.

---

**48. Can inventory be synchronized across multiple warehouses?**  
Yes. Khetify automatically synchronizes inventory across all connected warehouses, providing a unified view of stock availability.

---

**49. Does Khetify support batch and expiry management?**  
Yes. Products can be managed using batch numbers and expiry dates, enabling accurate inventory tracking and compliance.

---

**50. Will I receive alerts before products expire?**  
Yes. Khetify sends automated notifications before products reach their expiry date, helping you minimize waste and manage inventory more effectively.

---

**51. How does automatic reordering work?**  
When inventory falls below the predefined minimum stock level, the system can automatically trigger a reorder based on your inventory settings.

---

**52. How can I adjust inventory manually?**  
You can manually increase or decrease inventory through the **IMS** module. Every adjustment is recorded in the audit log to maintain complete inventory history.

---

**53. What are lots?**  
A **lot** represents a specific production batch or consignment of products. Lots enable complete tracking and traceability throughout the product lifecycle.

---

**54. How do I record inbound inventory (GRN)?**  
Receive incoming goods through the **Inbound** section and create a **Goods Receipt Note (GRN)**. Once the GRN is posted, inventory levels are updated automatically.

---

**55. How is outbound inventory managed?**  
Use the **Outbound** section to record product dispatches. The system automatically reserves and deducts inventory based on the dispatched quantities.

---

**56. Can I view my inventory movement history?**  
Yes. Khetify maintains an append-only inventory ledger that records every stock movement. These records provide a complete audit trail and cannot be deleted.

---

**57. What should I do if my inventory appears incorrect?**  
Review your inventory movement ledger to identify any discrepancies. If the issue cannot be resolved, submit a support request under the **Inventory & Stock** category for further assistance.
